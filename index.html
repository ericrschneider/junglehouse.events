About the Jungle House

Tucked away in Soquel, the Jungle House is surrounded by lush greenery and tucked under tall trees. We’re creating a space for music, conversation, and community gatherings that feel warm, intimate, and welcoming.

Our events are mostly small (usually 20–40 people), listening-centered, and often include time to connect with one another before and after the music or main experience.

If you’d like to hear about upcoming events, the best way is to join the invitation list above.

Meet Your Host

I’m Eric Schneider — musician, producer, and long-time house concert enthusiast. Over the years I’ve hosted many intimate shows and gatherings, and I’m excited to open up the Jungle House as a small, cozy venue for music and community.

This portal is a way for me to stay in contact with you and let you know about upcoming events without relying on the whims of social media algorithms.

I’m glad you’re here. I hope to see you at a gathering soon.
